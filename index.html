About Me

I am a Front-End Developer and Technical Writer with a strong academic and content-creation background. After nearly a decade in higher education and curriculum design, I moved into industry documentation, where I create user guides, online help, release notes, and training content, and I am now deeply focused on learning and implementing docs-as-code workflows using Git and static site generators.

As a developer, I enjoy turning ideas into responsive, accessible interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern tooling. I focus on building pages that are easy to navigate, fast to load, and friendly on any device, while integrating APIs in a way that feels seamless to the user.

As a technical writer, I specialize in explaining complex systems in simple, practical language. I also work with Snagit and Visio for visuals and diagrams, and I am building structured authoring skills with DITA-based practices, ensuring users can get the most out of software products. My goal is to make technology approachable and easy to understand for all users.
